6-Second Resume Challenge

2015 · UX & UI · web Design · visual Design

According to one famous study, hiring managers only take an average of six seconds to decide whether to keep or trash your resume. Is it true? We were all very curious about the answer so we created this campaign website.

We got inspired from this game.

I was the main designer of this project, I discussed with the business team and made wireframes to make sure we were all on the same page. Then I made mockups to ensure the look and feel. After we all agreed, we passed it to our developer to make it playable. And finally we launched the game and started to collect data.

With over 9,000 participants and counting, I created an Infographic to show the game results. The Infographic not only tells participants about the game result but also teaches them how to do to improve the resume.

The Director of Marketing on this project told me that this project was a big success because this is what is known as evergreen content, which means it will continue to bring traffic to our website long after the project has ended. This game also got a lot of attention on social media. Search for "6 second challenge” on Google and you will find plenty of links to this game!
