Homey Hostel Website

2015 · UI & UX · INTERACTION DESIGN · VISUAL DESIGN · RESPONSIVE

Homeyhostel is a popular backpacker accommodation in Taipei City. The client contacted me desiring a complete redesign of their existing website. The goal of the project was to improve online booking revenues while also more effectively representing the high quality and friendly atmosphere of the hostel.

A few weeks after we launched the redesigned website the customer reported that their booking conversion rates had increased from 1% to 3%.  As an avid traveler myself, and having stayed in many backpacker hostels throughout Europe and Asia, I was particularly attracted to this project.

The first step in this project involved the creation of a general set of wireframes to assist project stakeholders in better understanding the correlation between user experience and booking conversions. The next step involved the creation of responsive user interface designs, followed by close collaboration with the website’s developer to ensure accurate integration. of the design and its style guide.

SITEMAP

The client and I collected feedback and ideas from end-users, and then proceeded to make a second version of the website for the purpose of A/B testing. The result was that the second version lowered the website’s bounce rate by 10%.

The client asked some travelers why they chose to stay at Homey Hostel, and some of them replied that it was because “the website is beautiful, we can feel the owner’s attentiveness”.
